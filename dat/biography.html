This summer 2024 I'll be starting as a Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow at Arizona State University's Schoool of Computing and Augmented Intelligence. I'm wrapping up my PhD in Computer Science from UC Berkeley, where I've had the privilege of doing research at the RISE Lab and EPIC Data Lab. Adviced by Joe Hellerstein, my research has focused on experimentation in MLOps and the management of context in the machine learning lifecycle. This work unifies themes from data systems, software engineering, and applied ML. I am the chief developer and maintainer of the FLOR family of projects, a unified framework for managing the ABCs of Context in the machine learning lifecycle. I have been honored to receive the UC Berkeley Chancellor's Fellowship and the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship, further supported by a generous grant from Dell Inc.
