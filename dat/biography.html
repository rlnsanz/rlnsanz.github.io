I am currently an undergraduate research aide at the Visual Analytics and Data Exploration Research (VADER) Lab at Arizona State University. I expect to graduate May 2017 with a B.S. in Computer Science, and start my PhD in the Fall. I have been selected for honorable mention for the Computing Research Association's (CRA) Outstanding Undergraduate Researcher Award (2017).

The overarching theme of my undergraduate research has been to create technologies to help people understand and work effectively with large sets of data. My research exprience lies primarily in Visual Analytics, and Machine Learning. My goal is to study data structures, such as graphs or information networks, for fast machine learning at scale. I have presented three posters and given two talks at national symposiums and an IEEE workshop. My research has been published in the Proceedings of the IEEE VAST Challenge Workshop, and I have submitted two more journal publications on my most recent projects.
