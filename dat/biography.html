I am a Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow at Arizona State University's School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence. I recently completed my PhD in computer science at the University of California, Berkeley, where I studied the Management of Context in the Machine Learning Lifecycle. Advised by Joe Hellerstein, my research unifies themes from databases, software engineering, and applied ML.

I am the chief developer and maintainer of FlorDB, a hindsight logging database for the machine learning lifecycle. My PhD was funded by a UC Berkeley Chancellor's Fellowship and an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship, further supported by a generous grant from Dell Inc.
