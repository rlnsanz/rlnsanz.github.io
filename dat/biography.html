I am a fourth year Ph.D. student at UC Berkeley working in the RISE Lab. I work to help model developers learn from mistakes by leveraging context in natural and intuitive ways. I am advised by Joe Hellerstein. We've recently published a new technique called hindsight logging that enables model developers to "query the past" for any bit of training execution data. I am a recipient of the UC Berkeley Chancellor's Fellowship and the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.
