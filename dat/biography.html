I am a Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow at Arizona State University's School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence, where I extend my work on FlorDB (GitHub, YouTube) into the realm of document intelligence, building pipelines that log, query, and analyze document data with the same rigor as the machine learning lifecycle. Advised by Joe Hellerstein, I completed my PhD in computer science at UC Berkeley, where I studied the management of context (or metadata) in the machine learning lifecycle. My PhD was funded by a UC Berkeley Chancellor's Fellowship and an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship, further supported by a generous grant from Dell Inc.
